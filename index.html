System Components

Raspberry Pi Camera 3

12 MP, HDR, autofocus, NoIR option.

  • Resolution:12 MP
  • Sensor:Sony IMX708
  • Video:1080p @ 50 fps
  • FoV:75° / 120°

DHT11 Temp/Humidity

Accurate environmental sensing.

  • Temp:0–50 °C ±2 °C
  • Humidity:20–90 % ±5 %
  • Interface:Single-wire

SG90 Micro Servo

180° rotation for cradle rocking.

  • Torque:1.8 kg-cm
  • Speed:0.1 s/60°
  • Voltage:4.8–6 V

HC-SR04 Ultrasonic

2 cm – 4 m distance sensing.

  • Accuracy:±3 mm
  • Angle:15°
  • Power:5 V DC

Arduino Uno R3

Main control MCU.

  • CPU:ATmega328P
  • Clock:16 MHz
  • Flash:32 KB

ESP8266 Wi-Fi

Cloud connectivity module.

  • Standard:802.11 b/g/n
  • Freq:2.4 GHz
  • Range:≈ 100 m
